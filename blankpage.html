Consumer Protection – Telephone Consumer Protection Act

Are you sick of getting telephone calls or text messages from telemarketers and debt collectors? The Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA) was enacted by Congress in 1991 to protect individuals from unsolicited telephone calls or text messages. Since enactment, the FCC has broadly interpreted the law. If a violation has occurred, you may be entitled to recover $500 to $1500 per call or text made in violation of the statute. If you think this could apply to you, give Moore & Cross, LLP a call. You will not pay anything if we are not successful in obtaining a settlement or judgment in your favor.

Moore & Cross is here to remove those intimidating obstacles and ensure you are getting the best tax advice.
