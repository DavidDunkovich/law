About Moore & Cross

Moore and Cross was formed in 2018 under partners Austin Moore and Casey Cross. Both founding partners attended Northern Illinois University Law School, before each when his own way. Austin worked as an attorney for Schmiedeskamp Robertson Neu & Mitchell, LLP before starting his own practice in Quincy, Illinois. Casey began his solo practice shortly after becoming licensed in Wisconsin, operating his practice out of Kenosha and Walworth counties. Together they bring a wealth of experience and knowledge to provide excellent service to their clients.

Casey Cross

Contact
Casey Cross is a licensed attorney in the state of Wisconsin and founding partner of Moore & Cross, LLP. Casey earned his Bachelor’s degree in Finance from the University of Wisconsin-Whitewater before attending law school at Northern Illinois University, where he focused his studies on business and tax law. He also participated in the American Bar Association’s Tax Challenge, and worked as a tax clerk, while also serving as Treasurer and Vice President of the Federalist Society. Prior to joining Moore & Cross with partner Austin Moore, Casey ran his own practice as a sole practitioner. Casey’s practice included Business Formation and Management, Contracts, Real Estate, Tax Planning and Advising, Estate Planning, and Bankruptcy.
